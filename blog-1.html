The Future of International Development Industry

The international development landscape is undergoing a profound transformation. Traditional models of aid are being challenged by new financing mechanisms, a greater emphasis on local leadership, and the urgent need for climate-resilient solutions. At Evorise, we see these shifts not as disruptions, but as opportunities to innovate and create more sustainable, equitable impact. Key trends shaping the future include blended finance, adaptive management, and a focus on systems thinking.

Source: Devex, "The Future of Development Finance," 2024. | OECD, "Blended Finance Principles," 2023.
