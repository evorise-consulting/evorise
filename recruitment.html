Vacancy: Field Supervisor

We are looking for a Field Supervisor for a project in Maluku. Join us to lead survey activities in coastal communities. Deadline: 22 October 2025.

Apply Now

Details: Field Supervisor

Job Description

  • Supervise and coordinate a team of enumerators.
  • Ensure the quality and accuracy of data collected during surveys.
  • Conduct quantitative and qualitative surveys in coastal communities.
  • Report progress and challenges to the project manager.

Qualifications

  • Domiciled in Maluku (Ambon & Seram Island preferred).
  • Open to all majors, minimum D3/S1/Equivalent (Social Humanities preferred).
  • Experience as an enumerator or conducting quantitative & qualitative surveys.
  • Experience surveying coastal communities.
  • Accustomed to conducting surveys on small islands.
  • Willing to live on board (ship) if required for travel to TNS Islands (Teon, Nila, Serua).
  • Good communicator, team player, hardworking, and responsible.

Vacancy: Enumerator

We are seeking enumerators for a survey project in Maluku. If you have experience with field surveys, especially in coastal areas, we want you. Deadline: 22 October 2025.

Apply Now

Details: Enumerator

Job Description

  • Conduct field surveys in designated coastal communities.
  • Collect accurate data from respondents using provided tools.
  • Ensure all survey protocols are followed correctly.
  • Coordinate regularly with the Field Supervisor.

Qualifications

  • Domiciled in Maluku (Ambon & Seram Island preferred).
  • Experience as an enumerator or conducting surveys.
  • Experience surveying coastal communities.
  • Accustomed to conducting surveys on small islands.
  • Willing to live on board (ship) if required for travel to TNS Islands (Teon, Nila, Serua).
  • Owns a two-wheeled vehicle & SIM C license.
  • Good communicator, team player, hardworking, and responsible.
