Safeguarding and Child Protection Policy

Our Commitment to Safeguarding

Evorise Consulting is unequivocally committed to the protection of all individuals, particularly children and vulnerable adults, from any form of harm, abuse, neglect, or exploitation. This policy reflects our commitment to upholding the highest international standards for safeguarding, in line with the principles set by leading development organizations such as DFAT, USAID, the UN, and major international contractors.

Core Child Protection and Safeguarding Principles

  • Zero Tolerance: We have a zero-tolerance approach to any form of sexual exploitation, abuse, and harassment (SEAH), as well as any form of child abuse or harm.
  • Best Interests of the Child: In all actions concerning children, their best interests shall be a primary consideration.
  • Accountability and Transparency: We are committed to being accountable for our safeguarding practices and transparent in our reporting and response mechanisms.

Reporting and Responding to Concerns

Any individual can raise a concern without fear of retaliation. Concerns can be reported to our designated Safeguarding Focal Point via email at safeguarding@evorise-consulting.com.
